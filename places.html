Best Places

Jodpur, India

Jodhpur, known as the "Blue City" and "Sun City," is a historic city in Rajasthan, India, famous for its iconic Mehrangarh Fort and vibrant Rajasthani culture.

Jaipur, India

Jaipur, the capital of Rajasthan, is known for its stunning pink-hued architecture and historic forts, including the Amer Fort and City Palace. It is a major tourist destination in India.

Bikaner, India

Bikaner is a city in the northwest of the Indian state of Rajasthan, known for its impressive Junagarh Fort and its delicious traditional Rajasthani snacks, particularly Bhujia.

Jaisalmer, India

Jaisalmer is a historic city in the Indian state of Rajasthan, known for its stunning golden sandstone architecture and the magnificent Jaisalmer Fort, often called the "Golden Fort."

Ajmer, India

Ajmer is a historic city in the Indian state of Rajasthan, known for the revered Ajmer Sharif Dargah and the tranquil Ana Sagar Lake.

Udaipur, India

Udaipur, known as the "City of Lakes," is a picturesque city in the Indian state of Rajasthan, famous for its stunning lakes, palaces, and vibrant cultural heritage. It is a popular tourist destination and a symbol of Rajasthan's royal grandeur.

Alwar, India

Alwar is a city in the Indian state of Rajasthan known for its historical significance, including the Alwar Fort and Sariska Tiger Reserve. It is a popular tourist destination in the region.
