Best Food

Dal Baati Churma

A traditional Rajasthani dish consisting of spiced lentil curry (dal), baked wheat flour balls (baati), and a sweet crushed wheat dish (churma).

Gatte Ki Sabzi

Gram flour dumplings in a spiced yogurt-based gravy, often flavored with a variety of spices.

Ker Sangri

A traditional Rajasthani dish made from dried desert beans and berries, typically prepared as a vegetable dish.

Papad Ki Sabzi

A curry made with fried papads (thin, crispy lentil wafers) in a tangy tomato-based gravy

Aloo Pyaaz Kachori

A variant of kachori, which is a deep-fried pastry filled with a mixture of spiced potatoes and onions.

Pyaaz Kachori

Another variety of kachori filled with spiced onions, often enjoyed as a popular snack.

Methi Bajra Poori

A deep-fried bread made from pearl millet (bajra) flour and fenugreek leaves, served with a side of yogurt or pickle.

Kadhi

A yogurt-based gravy seasoned with gram flour, spiced with fenugreek seeds, and often accompanied by steamed rice.

Moong Dal Halwa

A sweet dessert made from yellow moong lentils, ghee, sugar, and flavored with cardamom and saffron.

Panchmel Dal

A nutritious and flavorful dish made by combining five different types of lentils, cooked together with aromatic spices.
